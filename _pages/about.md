Hi! I'm Carl, a PhD candidate in Bioinformatics at the [Pennsylvania State University (PSU)](https://www.psu.edu/). I'm very luckily advised by [Prof. Mingfu Shao](https://sites.psu.edu/mxs2589). Before my PhD study, I obtained my BS in Cell & Molecular Biology with First Class Honours from the [Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)](https://www.cuhk.edu.hk).

My research interest is in developing algorithms and methodologies for assembly problems and transcriptome bioinformatics research, including (allele-specific) transcript assembly, synthetic long reads, etc.
